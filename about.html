Hi! I'm Peter, a data-driven product designer with an educational background in architectural engineering. Currently, I work at Synapse to help solve design challenges in the B2B banking space. In the past, I've worked at an agricultural technology company, an architecture firm, and an event management startup. I believe good design empowers people to be the best version of themselves and I strive to enable that by being a relentless advocate of the user.

When I'm not designing, you can often find me doing something active outside. I spend my free time rock climbing, reading, kayaking, and traveling. Drop me a line if you'd like to connect!
